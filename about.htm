About us

Larry Akins Ventures Limited is A prominent Solid Minerals Company, fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. We hold licenses for both export and domestic supply, granted by the Nigerian Export Promotion Council and the Ministry of Mines and Steel Development. Our expertise, gained over many years of hard work and research, positions us as leaders in the origination, processing, and trading of solid minerals from West Africa. Larry Akins Ventures Limited specializes in a wide range of minerals, including silicates, certain carbon minerals, construction aggregates, and more. Notably, we are recognized manufacturers, exporters, and suppliers of Lepidolite, Lithium, Manganese, Charcoal, and primarily Mica and Cement.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure that we set a standard when it comes tosolid minerals exports and become the go-to brand for our international clients. We intend to become the number one solid minerals exporting company in Africa.

Mission

Our mission is to Provide the highest quality materials sourced under good conditions just as our motto will say “Quality commodities, responsibly sourced”. We intend to constantly deliver professional services, quality checks and export of solid Minerals. We provide these services whilst adhering to the economic, social and environmental conditions of our miners and host communities.
